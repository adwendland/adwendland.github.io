Research Interests

I am a PhD candidate in my fifth year at the University of Connecticut under the supervision of Dr. Xiaodong Yan. Research interests include applied and computational mathematics, partial and ordinary differential equations, the calculus of variations, numerical analysis, and materials modeling and simulation. Specifically, I am interested in PDE/ODE and variational problems motivated by problems in materials science, in particular liquid crystal theory for my dissertation work.
