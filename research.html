Research Interests

I am a PhD candidate in my fift year at the University of Connecticut under the supervision of Dr. Xiaodong Yan. Research interests include applied mathematics, partial differential equations, and the calculus of variations. Specifically, I am interested in PDE and variational problems arising from materials science and other applications.
